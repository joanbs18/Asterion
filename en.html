LEGAL PHILOSOPHY



"At Asterión RG Abogados, we embrace active listening, empathy, and personalized solutions as the pillars of our legal practice. Inspired by Jorge Luis Borges' work, 'The House of Asterion,' which illustrates how the same story can have multiple versions, we recognize that each individual has unique needs. In a legal world often characterized by coldness, complicated terminology, and generic solutions, at Asterión RG Abogados, we strive to change this narrative and evolve towards a more holistic and human approach. You can trust that we will navigate any legal labyrinth together that may arise, as we specialize in your unique situation."

Lic. Esteban Ramos Gutierrez



• Founding Partner of Asterión RG Lawyers.

• Former Judge of the Republic in Family Law, Domestic Violence, and Child and Adolescent Law.

• Mediator and conciliator in Labor Law, Certified by the Arbitration and Mediation Chamber (CAM) of the Bar Association of Costa Rica.

• Specialist in Notarial and Registry Law from the University of San José.

• Bachelor of Law from the University of Costa Rica with an emphasis on judge training.

• Officially incorporated into the Bar Association of Costa Rica.

WORDS FROM LIC. ESTEBAN RAMOS G.



FOUNDING PARTNER

• The world of laws and legal practice has always been characterized by coldness, complex terms, and generic solutions, which can be overwhelming for many people navigating a legal maze.

• My approach is different. I always dreamed of a legal firm that deeply values the story and context of each person we serve, aiming to provide tailored solutions and true expertise in every case. It was this passion for understanding and serving that led me to establish Asterión RG Abogados.

• My goal is for every person we serve to feel that we are more than just lawyers; we are genuine support, providing quality legal advice and assistance at every stage of their lives and in all the projects they wish to undertake. Ultimately, making all legal mazes more manageable and approachable for our clients.
